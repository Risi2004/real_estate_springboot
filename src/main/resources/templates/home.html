About Red Cardinal


With decades of combined experience in the UK property market, Red Cardinal uses its knowledge and expertise to present clients with the best investment opportunities across the country, to maximise returns.


By assessing and considering each individual client’s specific needs and goals, we determine the best location and type of investment, all presented in a precise but easy to understand manner.


Red Cardinal sources many properties throughout the UK, including key cities such as London, Manchester, Birmingham, Liverpool, and Leeds, we have personally invested in these cities ourselves. Our extensive experience in the investment process enables us to identify potential risks that may go unnoticed by others and filter out any misinformation prevalent in the property sector.
